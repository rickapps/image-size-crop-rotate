What is Fit to List?

Fit to List dramatically simplifies the process of preparing your images for listing online.  Crop, resize, and move to the next image with just one click. Batch resizing allows you to reduce an entire folder of images while you work elsewhere. No more squashed images! Fit to List understands some pictures are orientated vertically, others horizontally. It reduces them accordingly requiring no intervention on your part.

Save time with your scanner. Scan several items at once, then quickly separate those items in the scanned image with Fit to List.  The cropping rectangle retains its size for each image. Just move it to position and double click.  Was the item not lined up on the scanner correctly? No problem. Fit to List lets you rotate the image to make it level. Fit to List was designed specifically to help you get your images cropped and reduced in minimal time.
